Choir

A welcoming mixed voice ensemble

Led by our choral director, Philip Clieve, we cover a diverse repertoire of sacred and secular music, singing a capella and with piano, orchestral or chamber accompaniment. We perform four concerts each year including one "choral showcase" event. There are vacancies for all voice parts and we invite anyone with a love of singing to come and experience one of our rehearsals.
