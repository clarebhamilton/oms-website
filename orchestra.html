Orchestra

Great music-making in a friendly atmosphere

Under the baton of musical director, Jim Cooke, the orchestra performs a broad range of music from the great symphonies and concertos to the work of contemporary British composers, from film music to light orchestral selections. We give four concerts each year, three jointly with the choir and one exclusively orchestral concert.

There are vacancies for strings, especially lower strings, and for some woodwind, brass and percussion instruments.
